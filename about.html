Welcome To My Craft Beer Addiction

The Blackeye Collective serves as a platform for me to share my love of the craft beer movement and to hopefully engage more with likeminded members of the beer appreciation community.

A little bit about me. I have spent the last seven years employeed in the aviation industry working as a pilot in skydiving, charter flights and air ambulance across Australia, New Zealand and the Pacific Islands.

Before aviation I worked in artisan cafes, bars and coffee roasteries. Among other ventures, I roasted coffee in Wellington, New Zealand and opened a cafe with a view to setting new standards of quality for coffee in the blossoming coffee scene in Brighton, England. While I developed a love for quality wine working with sommeliers in the Supper Club in Melbourne, Australia, my true passion has always been craft beer.

After relocating to the Netherlands I found that I was spending all my free time exploring the Dutch brewing scene. The ability to bike around freed me up to explore local breweries and grow my interest in slow food (traditional, whole, locally-sourced foods) and beer. I have created this website to share my opinions on all things beer.

Get in touch and let me know if about your beer experiences and I will publish them here. With the Blackeye Beer Collective, I hope to contribute to an already thriving beer community, provide some helpful hints for follow beer lovers looking to check out Amsterdam and most importantly drink lots of good beer.
