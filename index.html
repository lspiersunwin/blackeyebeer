Oedipus Brewing opens Bathhouse Oedipus on the Javaplein

Its been a big year for the brightly colored brew crew from north of the river. Five years since the opening of the origional location a second site has been secured at the historic and iconic Badhuis. The Badhuis is an iconic building in Amsterdam East, built in 1941 when it functioned as a bathhouse for the neighborhood.
